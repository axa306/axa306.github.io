Hello, My name is Alexander Arevalo. I'm a senior undergraduate student at the University of Miami. I'm originally from the Washington DC area but moved to Miami to a few years ago to pursue my undergraduate degree. Some of my favorite hobbies inlcude traveling, hiking and practicing the piano. Having grown up in the Washington DC area, I enjoy watching the local sports team. I mostly follow the local football, baseball and basketball sports teams.

But despite having a favorite NFL team, I like to follow the careers of certain players throughout the NFL. I consider myself an avid fan of the sport in general and of the many great athletes whom play the game. With this website, I hope to give a summery of myself.
